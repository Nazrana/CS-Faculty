About Our Faculty

Introduction of Computer Science Faculty

Computer Science faculty of Herat University started its activity in the past as the Computer Department of Islamic University of Science and Technology, but due to the recent developments in the country and the rapid growth of information technology, this field attracted many interested people every year. With the increase in the number of students and the emergence of some problems, this university stopped operating in 2013 according to the order of the honorable Ministry of Higher Education, and after that it started operating again in the framework of Herat University with the help of Berlin Technical University. The Technical University of Berlin (TU-Berlin) in cooperation with the DAAD Institute and with the financial assistance of the World Bank came to cooperate in the establishment of the Computer Science Department by preparing the necessary facilities for this department. The Faculty of Computer Science joined Herat University since the end of 2004 according to 2004. This university has four departments of software engineering, information systems, network engineering and professional computer training.
