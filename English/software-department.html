Produce and provide engineering. The field of software engineering examines various aspects and needs of the social and economic life of society and creates software according to their needs.

Vision

Providing the necessary services at the regional and national level to meet the software needs of the society, considering the current development of network engineering, is the vision of the Software Engineering Department.

Mission

Providing standard education, teaching in accordance with current technological progress and market demand, building and managing software projects, preparing students to enter the labor market and work in companies, governmental and non-governmental institutions, the mission of the Software Department It is engineering.

Goals
  • Achieving scientific, professional and specialized self-sufficiency, moving towards productive knowledge, creating new and powerful capacities and raising the level of awareness of graduates for effective activities;
  • Acquaintance with new technological methods in the fields of software production, systems and technological service supply;
  • Quantitative and qualitative improvement of the department, scientific, professional and specialized guidance for students to find the necessary abilities for growth, development and sustainable development;
  • Paying attention to the state and level of knowledge of the people in order to consciously overcome the overwhelming influx of technology and its rapid descent within the society according to the needs and necessities created;
  • Plan development programs according to the spirit and desire of the people and the demand for time and effective investment for the timely achievement and growth of the country;
  • Presenting modern knowledge, to train and cultivate national cadres with working abilities and ready to serve the current society;
  • Preparing research works and group researches by professors and students to answer important and constructive questions and solve a number of scientific and technological needs of the society;
  • Increasing students' abilities to accept work in technical and research institutes and produce valuable scientific research works;
  • Improving the level of professors' knowledge by sending them to complete short-term and long-term programs (master's and doctorate)
