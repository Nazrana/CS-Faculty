Information Systems Department

About Department

Introduction

The age of technology is based on information and the way it is used in daily work. This has caused people to learn new technologies and always keep themselves up-to-date with the changes that have been made. Today, it is considered essential to record and maintain information in a manual and optimal manner and to prevent the possibility of tampering with it. With the progress of network engineering and the need to record and maintain information, it is very important and modern and appropriate methods have been created, one of which is having an information maintenance system or computer database. The database department is one of the most important departments within the framework of the computer science faculty and it tries to introduce the young generation of the country to the latest technologies in the field of information storage, maintenance and security. Therefore, this department has been able to prepare its students in both scientific and practical aspects in the field of technology by using the tools in hand for many years. During their four years of study, the students of the database department have learned how to design and build information systems and related software, protect data and databases, and use their knowledge properly, and after that they can become a source of service for their society.

Vission

Applying database topics in the database department, based on globally accepted standards that meet the needs of the Afghan society, and transforming the database law department into a department with expert staff, with postgraduate and doctoral degrees in the next five years.

Mission

The mission of the Department of Information Management is to prepare new teaching materials according to current technology, standard education, teaching according to the progress of current technology and market demand, building and managing database systems, preparing students to enter the labor market and work in companies. , government and non-government institutions.

Goals
  • Achieving scientific, professional, and specialized self-sufficiency, moving towards productive knowledge, creating new and capable scientific capacities, and raising the level of awareness of graduates for effective activities.
  • Getting to the corners of yesterday and today's society in the dimensions of information systems, familiarization with new technological methods in the fields of software production and electronic service supply systems.
  • Quantitative and qualitative improvement of the department to provide better scientific and professional services, scientific, professional and specialized guidance for students to find the necessary abilities for growth, development and sustainable development, keeping in mind the national development strategy of Afghanistan.
  • Presenting current knowledge for the education and training of scientific cadres with working abilities and ready to serve the current society of Afghanistan in the field of information systems.
  • Creating a scientific network with international institutions and domestic and foreign universities, for the transfer of global science and technology, using the experiences of national and international institutions and bringing modern technologies in the way of building a better future for our beloved country.
